Founders Story

During the GameStop saga, while the world was watching the short squeeze unfold, I was trading Nokia—one of the many stocks retail investors rallied behind. It wasn't just about stocks; it was about collective action, shared insights, and the ability of independent investors to move markets.

But as the dust settled, I realized something was missing. There was no dedicated space for retail investors to come together, share ideas, and make informed decisions without noise, misinformation, or institutional bias.

That's why I started OpenStreet—a platform built for independent investors, by an independent investor. A place where knowledge is shared, market trends are discussed, and smarter investing decisions are made—together.

This isn't just another investing forum. This is OpenStreet. The first real community for retail investors to stay ahead of the market.

No bots. No hype. Just investors helping investors find opportunities.
